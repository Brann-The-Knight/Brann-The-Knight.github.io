Home

A Knight at the Magician's Manor was an AR team project about a Knight venturing into a mansion to escape a storm only to be trapped inside by the Magician who lived within. Utilizing a set of cards scanned by the Player's camera could give the Knight abilities to explore the mansion, defeat the Magician & escape.

I learned that it is very important to work hard to get everything done early as bugs and problems would appear & could be addressed before the project end date. Making certain everyone turned in their work on schedule was very important, and work required for other members to build upon should be given priority in an individual team member's time management.

Building the cards required for the game allowed me to practice & improve my character art using Adobe Photoshop.
