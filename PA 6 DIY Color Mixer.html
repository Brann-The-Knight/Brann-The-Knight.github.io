A question for you

What kind of website would you make, when given the opportunity? To build a website, there were many different options that I thought of to build. Something suggested and well-liked by others, but not my favorite, was a Color Mixer.

What topic is your website about?

One thing brought up to me was a struggle to create a wanted color when China painting. My mother loves to China paint, and when the idea of this project first began, she recommended building an AI to help make the colors to utilize in her painting.

A struggle that she pointed out was needing certain colors to finish her China painting. However, she was unsure what colors to mix in order to get the desired color.

How then could she learn what to use to make that color?

Why was that topic selected?

This led to the idea for my final project: a DIY Color Mixer. It makes use of mixing the paints (or colors) that the user has on hand to create the wanted color. Originally, I did not plan to select this topic.

Other ideas appealed to me more than the Color Mixer. It was originally added to fill out the Proposal requirement amount at the time.

How does your form engage with that topic?

My form engages with the topic by first learning the proficiency of the user, to know what they are capable of. It then clarifies the color that they want to make!

The form then asks for what colors the user currently has available to make the final color, before finally asking for the length you want as a response. This response can be from one to three paragraphs to tell them what to do.

How does generative AI help you engage with your topic?

Generative AI helps engage with the topic by explaining how to utilize different paints that one currently owns, or specific paints needed to obtain in order to make the wanted color. AI then explains the amounts needed to be mixed in order to make the desired color.

It also suggests what colors can be bought to help create the specified color, or how to utilize already owned colors to make the wanted color.
