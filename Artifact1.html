Adventures of Sacha

The Adventures of Sacha was the first game I built. It is a platformer about a catlike being climbing a mountain to get food.

I learned a lot about stage building and my first try with coding, as well as creating all assets & animations.

That and building the cards required for the game allowed me to practice and improve my character art.

This project was enjoyable to build as I was able to take a simple idea & watch it come to life.

I used Unity to build the stage & animations. All artwork was created using Adobe Photoshop Coded movement for the player & enemies.
