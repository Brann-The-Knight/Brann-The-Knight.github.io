Home

The Meaning of Light is a point & click adventure team project created to teach about the meaning of light, and what it is. The main character is stuck inside of a building and chooses to take one of the two paths in front of them: light is a wave, or light is a proton. As the player journeys through each path they go through many of the same rooms that change between both sides. They meet a mysterious man who scorns the player; a friend who helps the player progress; scientists who argue about what light is; & a being made of light.

I have learned that scope is very important when building a project. If there is a problem causing quite a bit of trouble, handle it sooner rather than later. This project has helped me improve my coding skills.

I have enjoyed drawing the two scientists, building their characters & interactions with the player.
